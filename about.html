About Us

TPV Multiplex was formerly known as Shri Lakshmi Narayana theatre. Shri Lakshmi Narayana was erected in the year 1984 by a prodigious tycoon Mr.T.P. Velsamy Nadar who is also the initiator of Shri Jayalakshmi modern rice & oil mill. At the year 2007 Shri Lakshmi Narayana was the first theatre to familiarize qube digital cinema in South Tamil Nadu.
